- Track calories
  - Person's physical info
    - Height
    - Sex
    - Date of birth
    - Fitness level:
        - category: sedentary / moderately active / active / very active
        OR
        - mean daily energy expenditure
    - Weight entries: many
      - weight
      - date and time

  - Food intake
    - Individual food item macronutrients
      - Carbs (percentage)
      - Protein (percentage)
      - Fat  (percentage)
      - Common portions: many entries
        - name
        - weight or volume

    - Meal entries
      - Entry type: breakfast/lunch/dinner/snack
      - Foods: many entries
        - Food item
        - Amount
      - Date and time

    - Water intake
      - volume
      - date and time

- Track workout routines
  - Exercises
    - name
    - category
    - details
      - duration
      - intensity
      OR
      - rest period
      - sets (many)
        - reps
        - weight

  - Workout session
    - Date and time
    - exercise entries (many)
      - exercies + details
